Our Shop:

The Atmosphere

Welcome to our shop! The interior is made to be as comfy and cozy as possible, and we're more than happy to accommodate anything you need (blankets included, we understand) We open at around 6:30 for you early risers, and everything is coming out of the oven shortly before! Feel free to come in any time after that as we can guarantee it'll be warm and fresh for you. üåà This is a safe space for all.

Our Pastries

Our bread is made fresh every morning, and our sourdough starter is made and maintained by us! Making these wonderful pastries is one of our passions so please feel free to come in and try! We make danishes, croissants, and all sorts of bread! We do also make things as per a request if you'd like, although depending on the request we may charge an extra fee for ingredients.

Our Aesthetic

We're really proud of our storefront in this cute historical building. We've tried to put a focus on being ecologically friendly, as all packaging is biodegradable, we do not use plastics in any part of the process, and we also ensure that all ingredients are ethically sourced. We encourage biking, walking, or taking the bus to our shop if you can! We are perfectly situated along one of the many bike lanes in this area so please feel free to drop by and bring your bike in! We also have outdoor seating for the more city ambience inclined (or those of you who prefer the outdoors) And remember, ALL are welcome here.
